Math Reference Sheet

Area Formulas
Rectangle: \(A = lw\)
Triangle: \(A = \frac{1}{2}bh\)
Circle: \(A = \pi r^2\)
Trapezoid: \(A = \frac{1}{2}(b_1+b_2)h\)
Volume Formulas
Rectangular prism: \(V = lwh\)
Cylinder: \(V = \pi r^2 h\)
Sphere: \(V = \frac{4}{3}\pi r^3\)
Cone: \(V = \frac{1}{3}\pi r^2 h\)
Pythagorean Theorem
\(a^2 + b^2 = c^2\)
Special triangles: 3-4-5, 5-12-13
Circle
Circumference: \(C = 2\pi r\)
Arc: \(\frac{\theta}{360} \cdot 2\pi r\)
Degrees in a circle: 360°
The number of degrees of arc in a circle is 360. The number of radians of arc in a circle is \(2\pi\). The sum of the measures in degrees of the angles of a triangle is 180.
